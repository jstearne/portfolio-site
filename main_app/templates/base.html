All about Jared

I've worked in business-to-business sales my entire career, and I've enjoyed some success. But I realized that I like building things and I love learning new things even more, particularly when it comes to technology.

In 2020, I resolved to lean into what I really like and love. So I bought a used T-450 Thinkpad with way too little memory, installed Linux and hacked it to bits. And when I hit a wall in self-directed hobby-projects, I upgraded my laptop and signed up for a nights-and-weekends immersive software development bootcamp.

I joined General Assembly's Immersive Software Development course in May, not knowing a virtual enviornment from Vanilla JS. Now, I'm proud to see I've come a long way, and I'm even more excited to go further. In the past months, I've had a chance to build apps with a variety of languages and frameworks, all on my own or as part of a team. I know I can offer so much more, and I want to show it.

Technical Skills: Python, Javascript, HTML/CSS, React, Django, NodeJS, Express, SQL/Postgresql, MongoDB, jQuery, git, Heroku, REST API

Soft Skills: Communication, empathy, public speaking, presentation, content writing, note taking, delegation, task management
